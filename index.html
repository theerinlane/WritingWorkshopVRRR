The premise

Over these four weeks you will unearth yourself as a writer. You may begin them struggling to put pen to paper, but you will finish these four weeks needing a new notebook.

What's included
4
Weekly Workshops

Three-hour Saturday sessions. Bring whatever you're working on, or nothing at all.

1
Private 1:1 Session

A 30-minute conversation about the future of your writing and where you want to go next.

7
Writers Max

Limited to seven people. Intensive and intimate by design.

✦
A Public Reading

At the end of four weeks, you read your work out loud, in front of humans. Fear no longer suits you.

The arc
Week 1
Apr 25
Voice.

Find out what you actually sound like on the page. Not who you think you should be. Who you already are.

Week 2
May 2
Risk.

Write the thing you've been circling. Bigger, stranger, and more honest than you thought you were allowed to be.

Week 3
May 9
Revision.

Return to what you've written with new eyes. Shape it. Trust it. Make it yours in a way you can defend.

Week 4
May 16
Release.

Read your work aloud. In a room. To people who are rooting for you. You did the thing.

Who this is for

Everyone. Bring whatever you're working on. Memoir, poetry, a novel, a screenplay, a love letter cleverly disguised as a to-do list or… nothing at all.

Your instructor
